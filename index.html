Polished Copy (for your games)

Over the past few years I've been helping board game creators polish the rules, story, and other text (or "copy") in their games to get them ready for publication.
Some of the more notable games I've provided copyediting and proofreading for are Scythe, Red Rising, and Charterstone.

I've also proofread several books, including Myths of Innovation by Scott Berkun, as well as Kathy Sierra's Badass: Making Users Awesome.

"Ian is one of my go-to copyeditors and proofreaders for Stonemaier Games rulebooks. He has an exceptional eye for typos, stylistic consistency, and mechanical cohesion. He has worked on nearly all of our games, and I will gladly work with him more in the future."
— Jamey Stegmaier (Stonemaier Games)

Check out some of the games I've worked on, and get in touch if you'd like to talk more about polishing your game copy.
